ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Roshni and I double majored in Electrical & Computer Engineering & Computer Science at Rutgers University. Currently, I'm a Software Engineer at American Express. Previously, I interned at American Express, Colgate-Palmolive & worked at Makerspace where I helped other creative minds use the laser cutter, the 3D printer, and the use of the woodshop and textiles.

In my freetime, I like to create and design different pieces of art and experiment with diverse mediums to create experiences! I'm always working on new ideas/projects to challenge myself and ways to advance my skills. Currently, I'm learning WebGL to incorporate in new art projects!
